Coffee
    Our coffee is made from beans that have grown under perfect conditions.

Our coffee list includes the following coffees::
    Espresso
    Cappuccino
    Latte Macchiato
    Cafe Latte
    Cafe Mocca
Hot Cocoa
    We cook cocoa in the traditional way.
    Chocolate sauce is mixed with milk, after which we add cocoa if desired
Tea
    Our comprehensive range of tea includes, among others. black, green and yellow unflavoured and flavored tea, herbal tea and oolong.
    We use only natural aromas in flavored tea, and our staff is trained to brew the tea at the temperature they are intended for.
Other beverages
    Thanks to our A-licensing rights, we offer a great variety of red and white wines, as well as beer and cider.
    We also have other soft drinks.
Salty snacks
    We always make our buns with fresh bread and make very high quality slices and cheese.
    We also add lettuce, cucumber and tomato.
    We also offer savory pies, muffins and bagels.
Our varied menu
